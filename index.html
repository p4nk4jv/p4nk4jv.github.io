About me

I am a Cybersecurity Professional with over 3+ years of experience in the domain of Application Security Testing and Penetration Testing. I have a strong passion for security and have extensively worked on Web Applications, Mobile Applications, API, Thick Client Applications and Internal & External Infrastructure. I have also performed source code reviews on PHP, Java, and Python code to identify vulnerabilities and provide appropriate mitigations and high-level fixes.

In my current role at The SecOps Group, I lead a team of security professionals to provide a smooth functioning of a Penetration Test. I also work closely with clients to conduct security testing of the products and technologies, ensuring that their data is suitably protected and free from the risk of cyber-attacks and related problems.

I hold several prestigious certifications, including CREST Practitioner Security Analyst (CPSA), Offensive Security Web Expert (OSWE), Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP), eLearnSecurity Web Application Penetration Tester eXtreme (eWPTXv2) and eLearnSecurity Mobile Application Penetration Tester (eMAPT).

I'm also a Bug Bounty Hunter and received appreciation and Hall of Fame listings from various organizations, including Google, Dell Technologies, MasterCard, EC-Council, Comcast, etc. I'm also an active Synack Red Team (SRT) member and love to find security loopholes in the diverse range of applications.

In addition to my professional work, I enjoy sharing my knowledge and experiences with others. As a chapter lead at OWASP Indore, I love to interact with more people in the community to share and get knowledge from them.
